Everything your maintenance team needs

From reactive fixes to planned maintenance — one tool, no spreadsheets.

Work Orders

Create, assign, and track work orders through their full lifecycle. Status transitions, comments, time logging, and photo attachments — all in one place.

Asset Management

Build a full hierarchy of your assets — sites, buildings, equipment, components. Attach photos, documents, and QR codes. Import hundreds of assets from Excel in minutes.

Preventive Maintenance

Set schedules by days, weeks, or months. Work orders are created automatically when they're due. Never miss a service interval again.

Checklists

Define step-by-step procedures and attach them to work orders. Enforce completion — a work order can't be closed until every step is signed off.

Team Management

Roles for admins, supervisors, and technicians. Track certifications and skill expiry. Match the right person to the job automatically.

Guest Request Portal

A public URL for your tenants or guests to submit maintenance requests. QR codes on assets let anyone report a fault instantly — no login needed.

Reporting

Six built-in reports — PM compliance, work order trends, asset reliability, team performance, and more. Export to Excel for deeper analysis.

Audit Log

Every status change, edit, comment, and checklist completion is recorded. Full traceability for compliance-driven industries.
